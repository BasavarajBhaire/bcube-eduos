# BCube Gold Prompt™ Pre-Generation Validation Checklist

A prompt may be released to image generation only when every critical item passes.

## Critical — 100% pass required
- Exact book name, age level, page title and page number are present.
- A4 portrait, 210 × 297 mm, print resolution and safe margins are explicit.
- Official BCube logo placement is explicit; the age badge rule matches page type.
- Every required character is fully described in the prompt itself.
- Teacher–child interaction is explicit when required by page data.
- The child activity and protected response area are explicit and usable.
- All exact child-facing text is supplied; no empty or decorative speech balloons.
- No rule IDs, module references, file references, variables or placeholders remain.
- No contradictory layout, placement, colour, character or activity instructions remain.
- Negative constraints prohibit watermarks, unrelated logos, gibberish, unsafe scenes, clutter and distorted anatomy.

## Major
- One dominant educational focal point.
- Age-appropriate visual complexity and vocabulary.
- Teacher, parent and assessment guidance align with the objective.
- Star mascot canon is complete and consistent where Star appears.
- Text and activity zones remain free of obstructing artwork.
- White or approved pastel background and premium preschool style are explicit.

## Release result
- PASS: all critical checks pass and Gold score is at least 90/100.
- REWORK: no critical defect, but one or more major checks fail.
- BLOCKED: any critical check fails.
