# Nursery Print Readiness Status

**Status:** NOT APPROVED FOR COMMERCIAL PRINTING  
**Allowed now:** Internal digital review and limited physical proof copies  
**Authority:** BCube Publishing Standard (BPS) v1.0

## Current decision

The Nursery portfolio is not commercially print ready. Architecture, curriculum sources, production prompts, and governance are mature, but print approval depends on finished page artifacts and physical proof validation.

## Mandatory gates before commercial printing

1. Exact canonical book and page sources are locked to a repository commit.
2. Every required page is individually composed as one A4 portrait page.
3. The official BCube logo is loaded from a certified immutable asset.
4. Recurring characters and illustrations are approved and checksum verified.
5. All visible wording exactly matches the canonical source.
6. Page numbering, cover rules, margins, bleed, trim, typography, and colour policy pass technical preflight.
7. Every page passes BPS critical checks and scores at least 95/100.
8. The complete book passes sequence, metadata, copyright, publisher-identity, and consistency review.
9. A physical proof is reviewed for colour, legibility, trimming, binding, classroom usability, and child interaction.
10. Founder approval and a versioned release package are recorded.

## Current blockers

- Official logo certification is incomplete in the asset registry.
- Required recurring character and illustration assets are not fully Gold certified.
- Deterministic PNG and PDF rendering is not yet proven end to end against real production assets.
- Communication Champions has not yet completed page-by-page Gold approval.
- No complete Nursery book has passed final PDF preflight and physical proof approval.

## Approval rule

Commercial printing is prohibited until all gates above are complete. Documentation completion, prompt completion, or compiler architecture completion alone does not constitute print readiness.
