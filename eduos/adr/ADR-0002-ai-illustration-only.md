# ADR-0002: AI Is Limited to Candidate Illustration Generation

Status: **Accepted**

## Context

Whole-page image generation produced incorrect logos, invented text, inconsistent typography, wrong page geometry and multi-page composites.

## Decision

AI may generate candidate illustration assets only. Final logo placement, typography, page numbering, geometry, composition, scoring and release approval are controlled by deterministic software and approved registries.

## Consequences

- AI output must not contain logos or final page typography.
- Candidate illustrations must pass asset review before use.
- Whole-page AI output cannot be treated as a releasable page.
