# EX-ACT-001 — Nursery Gold Activity Page

## Page data

- Title: Colour the Big Ball
- Learning objective: Identify and colour one familiar round object.
- Competency: Curiosity, Communication, Fine Motor Control
- Child instruction: Colour the big ball.
- Teacher prompt: What shape is the ball? Which colour will you choose?
- Parent extension: Find one round object at home.

## Illustration specification

A friendly preschool child sits at a low activity table, holding a thick crayon near one very large outlined ball. The teacher kneels nearby and points gently toward the ball without touching the child's work. Star smiles from the side and gives an encouraging thumbs-up. Keep the ball and colouring space as the dominant focal area. Use a clean white background with only two small classroom cues in the distance.
