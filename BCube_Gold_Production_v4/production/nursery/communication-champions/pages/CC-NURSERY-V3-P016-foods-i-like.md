## Learning objective
Recognize familiar foods and discuss preferences.

## Exact visible wording
Circle two foods you like. I like ______.

## Child action
Circle two foods you like from six familiar foods, then say: I like ____.

## Observable evidence
Child identifies foods and states one preference.

## Teacher move
Name each food neutrally; do not label preference choices as good or bad.

### Teacher question
- Which food do you like?

## Parent connection
At mealtime, name one food’s colour, taste or texture without pressure to eat it.

## Locked composition
Six large separate food cards: banana, apple, rice, roti, carrot and curd/yogurt; Star holds an empty plate icon.

## Exact response space
Six circle targets and one oral starter.

## Page-specific prohibition
