## Learning objective
Identify common animals and enjoy coloring.

## Exact visible wording
cat; dog; cow; bird; Choose and colour one animal. It can ______.

## Child action
Name the four animals, then colour only the large outlined animal selected by the child.

## Observable evidence
Child names one animal and colours within one large outline.

## Teacher move
Name cat, dog, cow and bird; demonstrate one safe colouring stroke outside the outline.

### Teacher question
- Which animal will you colour?

## Parent connection
Notice one real animal safely from a distance and describe one sound or movement.

## Locked composition
Four small coloured reference animals above one large uncoloured friendly animal outline; Star holds crayons outside the work area.

## Exact response space
