## Learning objective
Recognize and practise waiting for a turn.

## Exact visible wording
First; next; then; last; My turn. Your turn.

## Child action
Number the four pictures 1–4 to show taking turns, then say first, next and last.

## Observable evidence
Child orders the turn-taking sequence and uses one sequence word.

## Teacher move
Discuss each picture, model picture 1 only and let the child order the rest.

### Teacher question
- What happens next?

## Parent connection
Use first, next and last while taking turns in a simple family routine.

## Locked composition
Four separated cards: children choose game, first child rolls, second waits, second rolls; same characters and board game throughout.

## Exact response space
Four empty sequence circles.

## Page-specific prohibition
