## Learning objective

## Exact visible wording
This is Star; Star is happy; Star wears a blue cape; Star helps friends.

## Child action
Point to Star, say three facts, then colour only the separate outline Star.

## Observable evidence
Child says one fact about Star and colours the outline without copying a completed answer.

## Teacher move
Model: This is Star. Star is happy. Star wears a blue cape. Invite the child to repeat or point.

### Teacher question

## Parent connection
