## Learning objective
Build speaking confidence through show-and-tell.

## Exact visible wording
This is my ______. It is ______. I like to ______ with it.

## Child action
Bring or point to one toy and use the three-step show-and-tell strip: name it, describe it, say why you like it.

## Observable evidence
Child gives one to three supported phrases about a toy.

## Teacher move
Model with one plain ball: This is a ball. It is red. I like to roll it.

### Teacher question
- What would you like to tell us about your toy?

## Parent connection
Practise the three short show-and-tell phrases with one household toy.

## Locked composition
One child holds a teddy before three picture cue cards: name, colour/feature, action; peers listen and Star indicates turn-taking.

## Exact response space
Three oral cue boxes; no drawing area.
