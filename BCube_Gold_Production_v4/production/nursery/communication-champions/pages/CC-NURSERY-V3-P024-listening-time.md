## Learning objective
Identify and demonstrate good listening behaviour.

## Exact visible wording
Look or face the speaker. Keep a calm body. Wait for your turn. I am listening.

## Child action
Circle the three pictures that show good listening, then practise the listening pose with a partner.

## Observable evidence
Child identifies listening behaviours and demonstrates one.

## Teacher move
Model eyes looking, body still and ears listening without requiring forced eye contact.

### Teacher question
- Which picture shows careful listening?

## Parent connection
Take turns telling one short thing while the other person listens and responds.

## Locked composition
Five action cards: facing speaker, quiet body, waiting turn, interrupting, looking at unrelated toy; Star models listening pose.

## Exact response space
Five circle choices and one practice prompt.
