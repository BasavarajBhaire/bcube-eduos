## Objective
Introduce speaking, listening, sharing and confidence.

## Exact visible text
- Listen with care
- Speak with confidence
- Share with kindness
- Feel proud and confident
- I can speak, listen, share and feel confident!

## Locked composition
Four clearly separated outcome cards: listening ear, speaking child, two children sharing, confident child. Star and teacher guide the eye path.

## Child action
Point to the four learning pictures, then repeat the model sentence with an adult.

## Teacher move
Introduce listening, speaking, sharing and confidence one picture at a time; pause for the child to respond.

## Parent connection
During one family routine, take turns listening and sharing one idea.
