## Learning objective
Express preferences and develop vocabulary through toys.

## Exact visible wording
Draw my favourite toy. I like my ______. It is ______.

## Child action
Draw or colour one favourite toy in the large space, then complete: I like my ____.

## Observable evidence
Child represents one toy and names it or states a preference.

## Teacher move
Show two familiar toy examples without choosing for the child; ask for one describing word.

### Teacher question
- What toy do you like?

## Parent connection
Choose one toy, name its colour and put it away together after play.

## Locked composition

## Exact response space
