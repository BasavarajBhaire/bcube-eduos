## Learning objective
Identify who belongs in a family and match words to people.

## Exact visible wording
mother; father; sister; brother; Draw a line. Say one word.

## Child action
Draw a line from each of four people to the matching word card: mother, father, sister, brother.

## Observable evidence
Child completes four matches and says one relationship word.

## Teacher move
Model the mother match only; read remaining words and let the child continue.

### Teacher question
- Who matches this word?

## Parent connection
Choose two family words and use them in short sentences about familiar people.

## Locked composition
Four large separated portraits across the top and four shuffled word cards across the bottom with clear straight matching paths; Star demonstrates one example outside the activity.

## Exact response space
Four uncluttered matching paths.

## Page-specific prohibition
