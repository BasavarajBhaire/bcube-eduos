## Learning objective
Describe an animal using size, colour, and movement words.

## Exact visible wording
My favourite animal is ______. It is ______. It can ______.

## Child action
Choose one of four animals and describe its size, colour and movement using the picture cues.

## Observable evidence
Child gives one to three describing words or a supported sentence.

## Teacher move
Model one animal only: The elephant is big and walks; then let the child choose another.

### Teacher question
- How does your animal look and move?

## Parent connection
Use two describing words for an animal seen in a book or from a safe distance.

## Locked composition
Four separate animal cards: elephant, rabbit, parrot, fish; cue icons below for big/small, colour and movement.

## Exact response space
Four animal choices and three oral starters.
