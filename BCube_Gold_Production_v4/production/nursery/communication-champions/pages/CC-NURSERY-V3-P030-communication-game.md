## Learning objective
Review speaking, listening, turn-taking, and polite words through play.

## Exact visible wording
Start; Listen; Speak; Take turns; Kind words; Finish.

## Child action
Move one token around a four-stop board: listen, speak, take turns, use kind words; complete the pictured action at each stop.

## Observable evidence
Child demonstrates at least two communication skills during play.

## Teacher move
Play with 2–4 children, read one stop at a time and allow gesture or picture responses.

### Teacher question
- Which communication skill did you use?

## Parent connection
Play a two-stop version using listen and speak during a family game.

## Locked composition
Simple circular board with exactly four large stops, one start, one finish, one movable token and Star as guide.

## Exact response space
One token path and four short oral actions.
