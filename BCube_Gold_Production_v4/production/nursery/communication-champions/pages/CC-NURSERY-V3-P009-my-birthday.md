## Learning objective
State age and connect numbers 1–5 with candles.

## Exact visible wording
How old are you? Circle your age. Add the same number of candles. I am ___ years old.

## Child action
Circle your age from 1–5, then draw or colour the same number of candles on the cake.

## Observable evidence
Child selects one numeral and creates the matching candle quantity.

## Teacher move
Count the first candle together; let the child continue and say I am __ years old.

### Teacher question
- How old are you?

## Parent connection
Count 1–5 safe household objects and say the final number.

## Locked composition
One large birthday cake with five dotted candle positions and a separate numeral choice row 1–5; Star celebrates beside the child.

## Exact response space
Five numeral choices and five unfilled candle positions.
