# Book Structure

## Total pages
44 physical pages.

## Architecture
- Opening and orientation: 4 pages
- Module 1 — My Creative World: 7 pages
- Module 2 — Shapes and Patterns: 7 pages
- Module 3 — Colours Everywhere: 7 pages
- Module 4 — Build and Create: 7 pages
- Module 5 — Creative Nature: 7 pages
- Module 6 — My Big Ideas: 4 pages
- Reflection and completion: 1 page

## Page roles
The sequence includes cover and identity pages, mascot/usage orientation, guided activities, open-ended challenges, reflection and completion.

## Standard activity page anatomy
1. Exact page title
2. One child-facing instruction
3. Dominant illustration or activity field
4. Optional Star mascot guidance
5. Teacher facilitation cue
6. Parent extension cue
7. Page number and BCube interior-page system

## Difficulty progression
Observe and copy → choose and complete → combine and transform → invent and explain.
