# Character Bible

## Star mascot
Friendly yellow rounded-point star, large eyes, small smile, simple limbs, stable proportions, encouraging gestures, never visually dominant over the activity.

## Children
Nursery-aged proportions, diverse appearance, natural expressions, safe posture, simple clothing, and no adult-like styling.

## Usage
Characters support instruction, confidence, demonstration, and sharing. They must not complete the child’s task for them.
