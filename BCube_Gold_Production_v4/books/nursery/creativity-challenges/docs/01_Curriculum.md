# Curriculum Framework

## Curriculum intent
Creativity Challenges Gold™ develops flexible thinking, visual imagination, constructive play, fine-motor control and communication through short, joyful tasks.

## BCube alignment
- Creativity: generate, combine and transform ideas
- Curiosity: notice possibilities and ask what else could happen
- Communication: describe choices, creations and stories
- Confidence: attempt, revise and share without fear of one correct answer

## Creativity pillars
### Imagine
Form mental pictures, predict possibilities and invent alternatives.

### Create
Draw, colour, arrange, connect, build and complete.

### Experiment
Try another colour, shape, path, material or solution.

### Express
Name, explain, show and celebrate a creative choice.

## Learning modes
- Divergent thinking
- Exploratory thinking
- Constructive thinking
- Expressive thinking

## Pedagogy
Activities use modelling, guided choice, open-ended response, repetition with variation and verbal reflection. Adult support should prompt rather than prescribe.
