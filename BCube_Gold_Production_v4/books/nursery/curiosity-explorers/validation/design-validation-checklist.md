# Design Validation Checklist

Apply to every physical page before artwork generation.

## Brand and header
- Use only the locked BCube logo asset; never redraw or approximate it.
- Interior logo position and size follow the approved BCube Gold template.
- Exact title matches the manifest and appears once.
- Title uses the locked hierarchy, colour, weight and safe-area alignment.
- Nursery badge appears on the cover only.

## Instruction and activity system
- One short, actionable child-facing instruction.
- Banner uses the approved border, radius, padding and spacing.
- Activity marks are large enough for Nursery children.
- No empty speech bubbles, decorative pseudo-instructions or dense adult notes.

## Layout and footer
- A4 portrait, 2480 × 3508 px target.
- Important content remains inside trim-safe margins.
- Page number visibility and value match the manifest.
- Footer treatment is consistent and does not compete with the activity.
- Illustration occupies the intended visual area without overlap or clipping.

Any deviation is `BLOCK` unless explicitly documented as an approved page exception.
