# Curiosity Explorers — Phase 4 Validation

Phase 4 converts the 44 Phase 3 prompt candidates into reviewed artwork-generation inputs. Automated checks are fail-closed and limited to objective properties; visual and educational acceptance requires human review.

## Gates

1. Prompt structure and identity
2. Curriculum and age appropriateness
3. Design-system compliance
4. Illustration completeness and consistency
5. Accessibility and usability
6. Publishing and prepress readiness
7. Human production approval

A page may enter Phase 5 only when every mandatory gate is PASS and its weighted quality score is at least 95/100. No automation may redesign, infer, reconstruct, or approve artwork.
