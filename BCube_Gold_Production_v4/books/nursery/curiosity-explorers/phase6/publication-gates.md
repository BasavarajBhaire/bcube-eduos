# Publication Gates

## Gate 1 — Artwork completeness
All 44 Phase 5 pages are human-approved and hash-locked.

## Gate 2 — Technical prepress
Dimensions, DPI, order, page count, safe margins, integrity and PDF rendering pass.

## Gate 3 — Editorial and educational approval
Titles, instructions, activities, age suitability and publisher details are verified.

## Gate 4 — Founder approval
The final PDF and representative printer proof are explicitly approved.

## Gate 5 — Release
The manifest, PDF, PNG package, QA report and archive are complete and versioned.

Failure at any gate blocks publication. Automated checks cannot grant founder or visual approval.
