# Phase 4 Release Gates

A page remains blocked until every applicable gate passes.

## Required gates
1. Curriculum identity: exact page, module, title and activity.
2. Prompt completeness: all 15 sections present and unambiguous.
3. Educational suitability: Nursery-level language, motor demand and cognitive load.
4. Design compliance: locked logo, title hierarchy, instruction banner, margins, footer and page number.
5. Illustration compliance: approved character system, required objects, no distortion or clutter.
6. Accessibility: large targets, strong figure-ground separation and no color-only instruction.
7. Print readiness: A4 portrait, 2480 × 3508 px, 300 DPI, opaque background and safe margins.
8. Human review: curriculum, visual and founder approval tied to the exact file hash.

## Fail-closed policy
Missing evidence, conflicting instructions, substituted assets, wrong dimensions or an unreviewed image blocks promotion. A successful automated workflow does not equal visual approval.
