# Prepress Checklist

## File integrity
- Exactly 44 physical page identities exist in sequence P001–P044.
- One individual PNG per physical page; no collages or contact sheets in approved assets.
- Files fully decode and contain no transparent or truncated regions unless explicitly permitted.
- Output filenames match the release manifest exactly.

## Print geometry
- A4 portrait: 210 × 297 mm.
- Target raster: 2480 × 3508 px with 300 DPI metadata.
- Trim, bleed and safe areas follow the locked template registry.
- No important text, page numbers, faces or activity marks enter unsafe areas.

## Visual and colour checks
- Text is crisp and readable at final print size.
- Lines and activity marks retain sufficient weight after print scaling.
- Colours remain distinguishable in standard commercial printing.
- No unintended sharpening halos, compression artifacts, banding or pixelation.

## Release gate
Prepress automation may verify files and metadata, but final colour, readability and visual integrity require human proof review. Missing proof approval blocks release.
