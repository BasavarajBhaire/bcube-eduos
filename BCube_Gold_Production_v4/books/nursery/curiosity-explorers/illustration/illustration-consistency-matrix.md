# Illustration Consistency Matrix

## Global visual language
- Clean white background with restrained pastel accents.
- Thick, rounded, even outlines; no harsh black masses.
- Friendly, expressive, age-appropriate faces and proportions.
- Soft, consistent lighting; no photorealism or cinematic rendering.
- One clear focal activity with minimal visual clutter.

## Locked recurring assets
| Asset | Required consistency |
|---|---|
| Star mascot | approved model, face, point count, eyes, gloves, shoes, cape and proportions |
| Children | consistent age, scale, eye style, hands, expressions and inclusive representation |
| Teacher/parents | warm adult proportions, consistent rendering and non-dominant role |
| Nature | recognizable forms, child-safe detail and consistent palette |
| Animals | anatomically recognizable, friendly and not humanized beyond the book style |
| Objects | large, unambiguous silhouettes suitable for Nursery recognition |

## Composition checks
- Required objects are present and countable.
- Similar objects share scale and perspective.
- Matching/choice items are visually distinct but stylistically consistent.
- No subject is cropped, duplicated accidentally, fused, distorted or hidden by text.
- Cover artwork is unique to Curiosity Explorers and not reused from another title.

A failed locked-asset or distortion check blocks production.
