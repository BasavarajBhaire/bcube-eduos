# Prompt Quality Score

Each page prompt is scored out of 100.

| Dimension | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Educational clarity and age suitability | 25 |
| Illustration completeness and specificity | 25 |
| Publishing and print compliance | 20 |
| Design and brand consistency | 15 |
| Production readiness and ambiguity control | 15 |

## Pass rules
- Minimum total: 95/100.
- No critical failure.
- No category below 80% of its available points.
- Human reviewer approval required before artwork generation.

## Critical failures
Wrong page identity; missing activity; contradictory composition; unsafe activity; missing print size; incorrect branding; collage or multi-page output; unapproved character/logo recreation.
