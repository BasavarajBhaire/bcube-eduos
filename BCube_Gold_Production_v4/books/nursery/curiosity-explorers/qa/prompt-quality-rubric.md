# Prompt Quality Rubric

Each of the 44 prompt packages is scored out of 100.

| Dimension | Weight | Mandatory evidence |
|---|---:|---|
| Educational clarity | 25 | objective, outcome, age-appropriate activity, success evidence |
| Illustration completeness | 25 | required subjects, actions, composition, exclusions and focal hierarchy |
| Publishing compliance | 20 | A4, 300 DPI target, safe margins, page identity and print constraints |
| Design consistency | 15 | locked logo, title, instruction banner, footer, typography and mascot rules |
| Production readiness | 15 | deterministic command, accessibility, negative constraints and acceptance criteria |

## Decision rules

- `PASS`: score 95–100 and no mandatory failure.
- `REVISE`: score 85–94 or one correctable mandatory failure.
- `BLOCK`: score below 85, conflicting requirements, missing page identity, unsafe activity, or invented branding.

A numerical score never overrides a mandatory failure. Human reviewers record evidence and corrective action page by page.
