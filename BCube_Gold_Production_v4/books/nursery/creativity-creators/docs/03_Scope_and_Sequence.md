# Scope and Sequence

| Pages | Module | Focus | Progression |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–4 | Opening | Book identity, child ownership, mascot and creative habits | Familiarisation |
| 5–11 | My Creative World | Marks, doodles, picture completion, imagination | Notice → imagine |
| 12–18 | Shapes and Patterns | Shape transformation, repetition, sequencing, visual construction | Copy → vary → design |
| 19–25 | Colours Everywhere | Colour choice, mixing ideas, mood and expressive colouring | Match → choose → express |
| 26–32 | Build and Create | Parts-to-whole thinking, arranging, constructing and redesigning | Assemble → transform |
| 33–39 | Creative Nature | Nature observation, texture, symmetry, organic forms and invention | Observe → reinterpret |
| 40–43 | My Big Ideas | Open-ended invention, story creation and presentation | Invent → explain |
| 44 | Celebration | Reflection, confidence and completion | Celebrate |

## Skill strands
- Visual observation
- Idea generation
- Pattern recognition
- Fine-motor coordination
- Spatial organisation
- Flexible problem solving
- Story and vocabulary development
- Creative confidence

## Adult facilitation sequence
Notice → ask → offer choices → allow experimentation → invite explanation → celebrate effort.
