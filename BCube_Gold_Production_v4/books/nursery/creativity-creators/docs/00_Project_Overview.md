# Project Overview

Creativity Creators Gold™ is a 44-page Nursery workbook for children aged 3+ in the BCube Future Skills Learning Series™.

## Purpose
Build creative confidence through open-ended observation, drawing, arranging, imagining, constructing and sharing.

## Educational position
The book treats creativity as a learnable process rather than an art-only outcome. Children repeatedly move through four habits: imagine, create, experiment and express.

## Design principles
- One clear learning focus per page
- Approximately 70% visual and 30% text
- Large activity areas and simple instructions
- Teacher-guided and parent-extendable experiences
- Inclusive characters, familiar objects and safe materials
- Progressive challenge without overcrowding

## Deliverable
A coherent 44-page commercial workbook architecture ready for production-prompt development under the BCube Gold Production Standard.
