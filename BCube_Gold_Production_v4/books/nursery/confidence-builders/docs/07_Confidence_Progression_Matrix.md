# Confidence Progression Matrix

| Stage | Pages | Focus |
|---|---:|---|
| Orientation | 1-4 | Safe introduction to confidence habits |
| Self-belief | 5-11 | Identity, preferences and strengths |
| Willingness | 12-18 | Trying, persistence and asking for help |
| Emotional courage | 19-25 | Feelings, voice and regulation |
| Agency | 26-32 | Choices and simple problem solving |
| Communication | 33-39 | Clear, kind and visible participation |
| Independence | 40-43 | Challenge, contribution and demonstration |
| Celebration | 44 | Reflection and positive identity |
